Meerkat Recording’s 10 yr anniversary compilation, Nomads in Sound 04

Stockholm based label Meerkat Recordings celebrates 10 years of dedication to the dub/bass/d&b/breakbeat continuum. With the Nomads 04 compilation, we follow the meerkats trails between steppes and burrows: in urgency, contemplation and collective action. The Meerkats have invited their favourite artists based in the UK, France, Peru, Germany, Sweden and the Canaries – with influences ranging from the Peruvian Andres to the Asian Underground. BASS is the common foundation on the journeys of the contributing artists.
