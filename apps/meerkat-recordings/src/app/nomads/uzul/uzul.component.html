How would you describe your musical background?
Live / Dj Producer & Bassmusic Addict.

What is your main musical influence and/ or collective?
I really like minimal & acid techno, dubstep trap music, dirty south, ulkbass.  Always looking for new trends !!

Can you describe the main milestones in your earlier production/ projects?
Musician and activist for 25 years in bass music, I participated or initiated several projects, KALYLIVEDUB (Jarring Effects / Pias) one of the founding groups of the dub scene in France with High tone (of which I was the singer before they started dub band). HYBRID SOUND SYSTEM (Sounds around) techno dub duo, which I started with the drummer from Kalylivedub and with whom I played in the rave party scene in the 2000s. UZULPROD (EXPRESSILLON / HAMMERBASS) hiphopnoise project created with Tit’o, the guitarist of PICORE. GRANIT (JFX LABS) techno dub duo with Molécule. 4GetMeNot  (Ukbass & Housebass side project ). At the same time, I continue my solo career under the name UZUL with which I released ten EPs with the labels EXPRESSILLON / MEERKAT RECORDINGS / JARRING EFFECTS / ODG PROD / MALLABEL RECORDING / I.O.T RECORDS dub, then dubstep then trap and now in techno dub I have been playing live for 20 years.

Which are your current projects and what inspires you the most at the moment?
I Just released in May 2021 a new album "Evolve" on the famous label Jarring Effects (LYON / FR) and therefore I am dedicating myself to the development of my new live for the postcovid tour !!!

Otherwise I am still hyper active in my new profession of trainer on my "Dub producer" training supported by Les escales Buissonnières and EMIL CENTER Which makes me the first French Dub teacher!

Which are your greatest passions as an artist?
Compose to play in live, live act is my passion in music !!!

What are your ideas behind your contribution to the Nomads in Sound anniversary compilation?
I’m participating in this compilation because I have already released a track on this great label, managed with class by my Swedish friends Anna and Peter. That I met at the Clandestino festival, on the lawn and in music!
